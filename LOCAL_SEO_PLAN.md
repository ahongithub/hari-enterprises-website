# Local SEO Plan — Ahmedabad / Gujarat

The biggest near-term win for a local B2B supplier is a well-optimized
**Google Business Profile (GBP)** plus consistent citations. Do not create fake
reviews or fake listings.

## 1. Google Business Profile
- **Claim/verify** the listing for Hari Enterprises at the Sabarmati office address.
- **Primary category:** "Refractory materials supplier" (or closest available,
  e.g. "Building materials supplier"). Add relevant secondary categories.
- **Name:** exactly "Hari Enterprises" (no keyword stuffing in the name).
- **NAP:** must match the website exactly:
  - Hari Enterprises
  - 13, Jay Laxmi Society, Nr. Vallabh Park, 'D' Cabin Road, Sabarmati, Ahmedabad, Gujarat 380019
  - +91 95588 19332
- **Description:** paraphrase the site's About copy — supplier/sourcing partner,
  since 2005, Gujarat + Pan-India, product families.
- **Products/Services:** list each product category (castables, fire bricks,
  ceramic fibre, insulation, anchors).
- **Photos:** real photos of stock, godown, deliveries, team (avoid stock imagery).
- **Service area:** Ahmedabad + Gujarat; note Pan-India supply where feasible.
- **Hours:** accurate business hours.

## 2. Reviews (organically)
- Ask satisfied B2B customers to leave a Google review after a good delivery.
- Respond to every review professionally within a few days. Never buy or fake reviews.

## 3. NAP consistency & citations
Keep NAP identical everywhere:
- Website (already consistent via `company.ts`)
- IndiaMART: https://www.indiamart.com/harienterprises-ahmedabad/
- Justdial, Sulekha, TradeIndia, ExportersIndia (India B2B directories)
- Local Ahmedabad business directories
Audit periodically for mismatches (old numbers/addresses hurt ranking).

## 4. IndiaMART alignment
- Ensure IndiaMART name/address/phone match the website and GBP exactly.
- Link the website from IndiaMART where possible; the site links to IndiaMART as
  a secondary trust channel (already implemented in header/footer/contact).

## 5. Local landing content
- Home and product pages already carry natural Ahmedabad/Gujarat context.
- Optionally add city context in product copy over time (e.g. supply to Vatva,
  GIDC estates) — only where true.

## 6. Search Console & Analytics
- Add and verify the domain in Google Search Console; submit `sitemap.xml`.
- Track impressions/clicks for target keywords; refine titles/descriptions.
- See `ANALYTICS_SETUP.md` for GA4/Clarity.

## 7. Avoid local-SEO spam
- No fake reviews, no keyword-stuffed business name, no fake secondary listings,
  no invented addresses. These risk suspension and are against Google's policies.
